From symptom to specialist

One device. Four decisive steps.

Every interaction moves a patient closer to care, even where no doctor is present and no signal reaches.

01

Assess

A first responder describes symptoms in their own language. The model interprets and probes for what matters.

02

Recommend

Instant first-aid guidance or an emergency protocol, ranked by urgency and grounded in clinical evidence.

03

Route

The nearest qualified doctor, hospital, or specialist — with contact details and turn-by-turn directions.

04

Reach

The patient arrives at care faster — the gap between an emergency and a clinician closed in minutes, not days.

Built for where signal fails

Most apps stop when the network does. Ours doesn't.

Unlike telemedicine that assumes stable connectivity, MediReach runs diagnostic models locally on low-power edge devices. Care happens in the moment, on the ground — and syncs when a connection returns.

Engineered on NVIDIA

Lightweight at the edge. Scalable at the hub.

A layered architecture that runs where the patient is and scales where the data lives.

Edge inference

Jetson modules

NVIDIA Jetson edge AI runs lightweight diagnostic models locally on low-power devices — in clinics, ambulances, and the field, no datacenter required.

Cloud scale

NIM microservices

NVIDIA NIM microservices deliver scalable cloud inference at central hubs, handling heavier workloads and coordinating across regions.

Model training

BioNeMo framework

NVIDIA BioNeMo accelerates clinical NLP training on multilingual, low-resource datasets — so the model speaks the languages of the unreached.
