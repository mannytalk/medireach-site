MediReach AI bridges the healthcare gap in the Global South by delivering AI-powered triage and diagnosis to first responders, rural clinics, and mobile ambulances with zero specialist dependency. Our platform assesses symptoms, recommends first aid or emergency protocols, and instantly routes patients to the nearest qualified doctor, hospital, or specialist — complete with contact details and turn-by-turn directions. We target underserved communities across Africa, South Asia, and Latin America where 1 billion+ people lack access to basic diagnostics. Unlike telemedicine apps requiring stable connectivity, MediReach works offline-first. We leverage NVIDIA's Jetson edge AI modules to run lightweight diagnostic models locally on low-power devices in remote settings, and NVIDIA NIM microservices for scalable cloud inference at central hubs. NVIDIA's BioNeMo framework accelerates our clinical NLP model training on multilingual, low-resource datasets. Our mission: Reach the Unreached. Treat the Untreated.
